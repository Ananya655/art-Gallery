Upcoming Paintings Events Over the World!

"American Photography" at the Rijksmuseum, Amsterdam (February 1 – June 9, 2025): This exhibition presents over 200 works by iconic American photographers, including Nan Goldin, Andy Warhol, Diane Arbus, and Richard Avedon, offering insights into American life through diverse perspectives. "Anselm Kiefer" at the Stedelijk Museum, Amsterdam (March 7 – June 9, 2025): A comprehensive showcase of Anselm Kiefer's works, including the iconic "Innenraum," with both new and previously unseen pieces, providing a deep dive into the artist's exploration of history and memory.
"The Stars We Do Not See: Australian Indigenous Art" at the National Gallery of Art, Washington D.C. (2025): Featuring over 200 works from the 19th century to the present, this exhibition highlights the richness and diversity of Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander art, including pieces by Emily Kam Kngwarray and Albert Namatjira. Liverpool Biennial 2025 (June 7 – September 14, 2025): Titled 'BEDROCK,' the 13th edition of the Liverpool Biennial draws inspiration from the city's sandstone geography, featuring contemporary artworks that explore themes of identity, community, and place.

About Reva

Reva is a virtual art gallery dedicated to promoting artistic expression and making art accessible to everyone around the world.

Founded in 2023, we've grown to showcase over 10,000 artworks from 2,500+ artists globally.
